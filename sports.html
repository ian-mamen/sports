Different types of sports appeal to different types of people

Sports is a form of physical activity that involves using different movements to achieve a task.

They are classified as either of the two

  1. Track
  2. Field

Track activities are usually performed on a pathway designed for runners and include the following:

  • Running
  • Walking Races

Field events are usually performed on a larger area in length and width and they can include:

  1. Hockey
  2. Discus
  3. Spear Throw
